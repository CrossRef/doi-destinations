Every DOI resolves to a URL. Knowing where DOIs point can help identifying scholarly content where the DOI isn't known.

We take a representative sample of DOIs from each Crossref member and resolve them to see where they lead. Sometimes a publisher website can redirect several times. We store the first and last redirect.

As the data is taken from a sample of DOIs, we can't guarantee that the coverage is perfect. There may be publisher domains that we haven't covered. The data is continually updated.
